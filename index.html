Our Specialization

What We Do

Railings

Custom MS/SS railings for balconies, stairs, and terraces—engineered for safety, clean detailing, and long-term durability.

Gates (Sliding, Swing, Telescopic)

Secure, precision-built gates with robust frames and hardware alignment; optional automation and access control integration.

Metal Staircases

Straight, spiral, helical, and cantilevered stairs—site-fit installation with coordinated treads, handrails, and glass/wood accents.

Elevations & Facades

Feature screens, louvers, and façade frames that enhance curb appeal and withstand weather with durable finishes.

Gazebos & Pergolas

Outdoor structures in MS/SS/aluminum with powder-coat or galvanizing—designed for shade, style, and longevity.

Carports

Durable metal carports with engineered frames and weather‑resistant roofing—optional gutters, lighting, and solar integration.

Metal Ceilings

Linear baffles, open-cell, clip-in, and custom perforated panels—functional performance with refined aesthetics.

Metal Partitions

Fixed or modular MS/SS frames with glass, mesh, or solid panels—clean lines, durable finishes, and quick installation.

Industrial Sheds

Portal frames, purlins, decking, and roofing—optimized for span, wind loads, and rapid on-site assembly.

Solar Mounting Structures

Rooftop and ground-mount PV racking—custom brackets, rails, and frames with corrosion‑resistant finishes.

Custom Furniture & Decor

Metal-centric tables, consoles, partitions, planters, and feature pieces—crafted to complement interior and exterior spaces.

Custom Metal Fabrication

Bespoke frames, platforms, displays, enclosures, and project-specific metalworks tailored to your requirements.
